About Project

Why this problem matters

What is LISS-IV?

LISS-IV is a high-resolution multispectral imaging sensor used for detailed Earth observation, mapping, and resource monitoring across diverse landscapes.

Why cloud removal is important

Cloud cover blocks critical terrain information, reducing the usefulness of satellite imagery for planning, monitoring, and rapid-response decision making.

How Generative AI helps

Generative models can infer plausible surface structure from surrounding context, supporting clearer interpretation when direct observation is unavailable.
