Establishment, mission, headquarters, and genesis

This section summarizes key official ISRO background details and links each topic back to the relevant source page for deeper reading.

Establishment

Timeline from INCOSPAR to ISRO

Official ISRO pages note that INCOSPAR was set up in 1962 under the vision of Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, ISRO was formed on August 15, 1969, and the Department of Space was established in June 1972 before ISRO was brought under DoS in September 1972.

Read the official genesis page

Headquarters

Bengaluru-led national network

ISRO headquarters are in Bengaluru, while launch, satellite, propulsion, applications, and remote sensing work is distributed across multiple centres and units around India.

Open official profile

Mission

Space technology for national development

The official vision focuses on harnessing, sustaining, and expanding space technology for national development while continuing space science and planetary exploration.

Genesis

Science, communication, and societal applications

ISRO's genesis story emphasizes early communication experiments, remote sensing, launch systems, and public-use applications that connected space work to real national needs.
